# 🚀 SurakshaPay – AI-Powered Income Protection for Gig Workers

## 📌 Problem Statement
India’s gig workers (Zomato, Swiggy, Amazon, Zepto, etc.) often lose **20–30% of their weekly income** due to uncontrollable external disruptions such as:

- 🌧️ Extreme weather
- 🌫️ High pollution (AQI)
- 🚦 Traffic congestion
- 🛑 City restrictions / shutdowns

Currently, there is **no financial safety net** for this income loss.

---

## 💡 Our Solution
**SurakshaPay** is an **AI-powered parametric insurance platform** that:

- Predicts risk using environmental data
- Calculates **weekly premiums**
- Automatically detects disruptions
- Triggers **instant payouts without manual claims**

---

## 🧠 Key Features

### 🔹 AI Risk Assessment
- Predicts disruption risk using:
  - Weather data 🌧️
  - AQI levels 🌫️
  - Traffic conditions 🚦
- Generates a **risk score (0–100)**

---

### 🔹 Weekly Pricing Model
- Designed for gig workers’ earning cycle
- Example:
  - ₹50/week → coverage up to ₹2000

---

### 🔹 Parametric Insurance Automation
- Real-time monitoring of:
  - Rainfall
  - Pollution
  - Traffic
- Automatically triggers:
  - Claim generation
  - Payout processing

---

### 🔹 Fraud Detection (AI-Based)
- Detects:
  - False claims
  - Duplicate claims
  - Location mismatch
- Uses anomaly detection logic

---

### 🔹 Smart Dashboard
- Weekly risk prediction
- Active coverage status
- Past payouts
- Visual analytics
